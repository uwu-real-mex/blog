Ingredients

  • 6 cups chopped, fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 (10 3/4-oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 stick butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups grated extra-sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 full sleeve Ritz crackers, finely crushed

instructions

  1. Place broccoli in a steamer basket over simmering water. Cover and steam for approximately 5 minutes.
  2. Chop into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  4. In a large bowl, combine broccoli, soup, mayonnaise, butter, eggs, onion, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Add 1 cup of cheese and mix again.
  7. Place mixture in a medium buttered casserole dish.
  8. Sprinkle remaining cheese and crackers on top.
  9. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes and let stand for 15 minutes before serving
